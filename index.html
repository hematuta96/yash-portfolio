| ABOUT ME |

My journey as a film producer is driven by a passion for storytelling, creativity, and bringing powerful cinematic visions to life.

As a producer, I have worked across various stages of film production, from concept development and project planning to execution and post-production. My focus is on creating meaningful stories that connect with audiences and leave a lasting impact.

Through dedication, collaboration, and attention to detail, I strive to transform ideas into memorable visual experiences. Every project is an opportunity to explore new perspectives and push creative boundaries.

  • Produced multiple independent and creative film projects.
  • Specialized in production planning and project management.
  • Experienced in coordinating creative teams and resources.
  • Passionate about innovative storytelling and visual excellence.

2016

Pellichoopulu

ROMANTIC COMEDY | PRODUCER

Production: Big Ben Cinemas

Produced by Yash Rangineni, Pellichoopulu became one of the most celebrated Telugu films of the decade, introducing fresh talent and winning prestigious national recognition.

  • National Film Award – Best Feature Film (Telugu)
  • Filmfare Award – Best Film Telugu
  • Nandi Award – Best Feature Film
2019

Dear Comrade

DRAMA / ROMANCE | PRODUCER

Produced by Yash Rangineni under Big Ben Cinemas, Dear Comrade became one of the most talked-about Telugu films, praised for its emotional storytelling, powerful performances, and memorable music.

Released in Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, and Kannada, the film connected with audiences through its powerful performances, memorable music, and meaningful social message. Dear Comrade further strengthened Big Ben Cinemas' reputation for supporting quality-driven and impactful cinema.

  • Multi-language theatrical release
  • Starring Vijay Deverakonda & Rashmika Mandanna
  • Critically acclaimed soundtrack
2023

Annapurna Photo Studio

COMEDY / DRAMA | PRODUCER

Annapurna Photo Studio is a heartwarming Telugu comedy-drama produced by Yash Rangineni under the Big Ben Cinemas banner. Set in the 1980s, the film combines nostalgia, humor, and emotion while telling a unique story.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS

  • Released in 2023 under Big Ben Cinemas
  • Celebrated for its nostalgic storytelling
  • Praised for cinematography and visual style

Dorasani

PERIOD ROMANTIC DRAMA | PRODUCER

Set against the backdrop of rural Telangana in the 1980s, Dorasani tells a poignant love story that explores social divisions, tradition and youthful dreams. The film was appreciated for its authentic setting, emotional storytelling and memorable performances.

2019

ABCD

COMEDY DRAMA | PRODUCER

ABCD follows the journey of an affluent young man who is forced to adapt to life in India after losing access to his luxurious lifestyle. Blending humor, family values and personal growth, the film connected with younger audiences and showcased a refreshing coming-of-age story.
